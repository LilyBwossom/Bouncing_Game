--controls--
WASD to move.
Space to jump.

--general mechanics--
Spikes kill you.
Ice is slippery.
Coins can be collected.
The flag brings you to the next level.
The fan blows upwards.
The apple gives you a jump boost for 5 seconds (and respawns after you die).
The banana gives you a speed boost for 5 seconds (and respawns after you die).
